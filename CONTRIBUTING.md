# Contributing to Skynet Paperclip

## How to propose changes

1. **Create a branch** from `main`
2. **Make your changes** and commit with clear messages
3. **Open a pull request** with a description of what changed and why
4. **Request review** from `@ArtificialJerome` (see CODEOWNERS)
5. **Wait for approval** — at least 1 approval required before merge

## Merge process

- **No direct pushes to `main`** — all changes go through PRs
- **Require 1 approval** before merging
- **Use squash-merge** to keep history clean

## Submitting PRs

Include in your PR description:

- What changed
- Why (motivation/context)
- Any impact on sync scripts or live environment
- Verification steps (tests, manual verification)

## Questions?

Open an issue or ask in the PR. For urgent matters, reach out to Jerome directly.
